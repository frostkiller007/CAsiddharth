Registration of Indian Subsidiary Company

REGISTRATION OF INDIAN SUBSIDIARY COMPANY BY FOREIGN PROMOTORS

Incorporation of Indian subsidiary in India by foreign promotors is best and popular option to start business in Indian. Indian subsidiary company can be easily incorporated in India and there are no need to visit in India by foreign promoters. Foreign company can hold 100% shares of Indian subsidiary company subject to FDI limit however lots and area are come under 100% FDI limit to run the business in India.

ADVANTAGE TO OPEN INDIAN SUBSIDIARY IN INDIAN BY FOREIGN PROMOTORS
To Access fast growing Indian Market.
Ease of doing business in India.
Lower taxation applicable to private limited company.
Easy Process of Subsidiary Incorporation.
100% FDI are permissible in popular sectors.
Limited Liability.
Purchase of property in India.

REQUIREMEMTS TO FORM A INDIAN SUBSIDIARY COMPANY IN INDIA
Minimum Two Director required and at least one director should be the resident of India.
Minimum Two Shareholders required.
Foreign Promoters can hold 100% share of Indian subsidiary.
No Minimum capital Requirement for form a company.
One registered office required to be shown as registered office of the company.

It is suitable when the purpose is to render services or sell products manufactured by foreign entity by establishing its presence in India.

DOCUMENTS REQUIREMENTS TO FORM A INDIAN SUBSIDIARY COMPANY IN INDIA BY FOREIGN PROMOTORS

Documents Requirements For Indian Nations

PAN Card.
Aadhar Card.
Photo in Jpeg.
ID Proof (copy of Either Voter's ID or Passport or Driver's License).
Address Proof (Either Bank Statement or Telephone or Mobile Bill or Electricity).

Documents Requirements For Foreign Nations

Passport Copy.
Address Proof. (Either Bank Statement or Telephone or Mobile Bill or Electricity).
If the shareholder is foreign company then MOA, AOA and certification of Incorporation of foreign company.
Board Resolution to appoint authorised representative and nominee in Indian subsidiary.

Please Note that in the case of MOA, AOA, certificate of Incorporation, board resolution, passport copy and address prof should be in English language and duly appostile and notarised in home country.
